Daisy Dirt's dad is a dustman on the dole, but her mum has remarried a Duke,  so she's a filthy rich Duchess. Poor Daisy doesn't know whether she's coming  or going, whether she's rich or poor - until everything changes. Based on  the classic 'Happy Families' card game, this highly entertaining series is  ideal for reading and sharing at home or at school. It is guided by the  Education Adviser, Brian Thompson, and written by the award-winning author,  Allan Ahlberg. 'The best thing to happen to beginner readers since Dr Seuss'  - Children's Rights Workshop.
