Why do dogs wag their tails? How should you prepare for a new puppy? How do you train a dog to sit and stay?The Dog Lover's Guide will answer all your dog-related questions!From house training to playing games, this guide contains everything you need to know about caring for and understanding your dog. As well as chapters on dog care, how to speak dog and training and tricks, this book also features an alphabetical breed section.Beautiful photographs of the most popular dog breeds from around the world accompany key facts about each breed, helping readers decide on the perfect pet.
