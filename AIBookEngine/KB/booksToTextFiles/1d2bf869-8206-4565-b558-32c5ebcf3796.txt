A long time ago a little girl named Lola lived. Lola and her family belonged to the Selk'nam tribe, who settled in a beautiful country near the end of the world. One day, when Lola is in the south of the country with the Yamana tribe, a large ship reaches her country. Will the people on board change Lola's land forever?The new Helbling Young Readers series is aimed at primary school children and offers captivating reading, a multimedia CD-ROM and a magnificent picture book in one. Specially designed for early English lessons, the texts are carefully divided into 5 levels of difficulty, based on the Cambridge Young Learners English tests.The stories are written especially for young English learners. Frequent and important words were given special consideration in order to promote a natural and fluent way of expression from the beginning.
