THE MURDER OF ROGER ACKROYD - R&T 3 (B1.2)The famous detective Hercule Poirot is resting in the small English town of King's Abbot. Unfortunately, every town hides secrets and when his friend Roger Ackroyd is murdered, Poirot must solve one of the strangest and most difficult mysteries of his career. The Murder of Roger Ackroyd has been defined as Agatha Christie's best novel and one of the best crime novels of all time.
