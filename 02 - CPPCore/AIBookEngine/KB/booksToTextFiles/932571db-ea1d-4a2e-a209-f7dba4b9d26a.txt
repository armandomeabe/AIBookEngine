Introducing Alice the Amber Fairy, the latest addition to the Sparkle Town Fairies series.Meet Alice, owner of the Amber Music Emporium and Sparkle Town's amazing amber fairy. Alice loves using her amber wand to invent glittering new musical instruments for the fairies of Sparkle Town. This year, Alice is invited to enter the town's musical extravaganza: the Showstopper Spectacular! Everyone assumes Alice will win, so desperate not to let anyone down, she tries to invent the most incredible instrument ever. But when things don't go to plan, can Alice make the right decision and still win the prize?Beautiful illustrations and funny, rhyming text make this story a delight to read with one child or with a group.
