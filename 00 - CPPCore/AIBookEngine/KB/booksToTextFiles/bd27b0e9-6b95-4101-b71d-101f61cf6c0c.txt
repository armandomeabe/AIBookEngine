This award-winning series has been revised to meet the Australian Currciculum requirements, providing a context-based approach, demonstrating how grammar works at the word, sentence and text levels to communicate meaning. The new edition of Grammar Rules! provides a whole-school program that is systematic, purposeful and sequential. There are resources for whole-class modelling and instruction and more assessment tools for feedback to teachers and students. The series includes:Easy to understand rule and tips for new concepts and reminders.'Try it yourself' sections to encourage students to apply learning in their own writing.Teaching tools on disc for whole-class instruction.Unit-by-unit teaching notes for use with the student books.
