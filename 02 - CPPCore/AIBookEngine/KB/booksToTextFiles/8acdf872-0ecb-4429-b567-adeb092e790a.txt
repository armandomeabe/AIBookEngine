THE LADY IN WHITE    Award-winning original fiction for learners of English. Is it coincidence or  is he losing his mind? While researching a new programme, John, a successful  television producer, comes across a story about a ghostly hitch-hiker. Soon  he realises that the story of the Lady in White bears many similarities to  part of his own life. Returning to London from a holiday in Ireland, John  begins to ask himself some uncomfortable questions.
