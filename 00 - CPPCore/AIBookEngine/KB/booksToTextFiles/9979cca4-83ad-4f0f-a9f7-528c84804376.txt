A CHRISTMAS PRESENT FOR BARNEY BUNNY - HYRF BIt's Christmas Eve and Mother Bunny is busy getting everything ready for Christmas. The bunnies aren't helping so she sends them out to play. Barney, the baby bunny, can't play any of the games. But on Christmas Day Mother Bunny has a special present for him and all her other children.Series: Helbling Young Readers FictionSeries Level: Level BIllustrator: Lorenzo SabbatiniSchool Type: Primary SchoolLanguage Level: Cambridge Pre-A1 Starters
