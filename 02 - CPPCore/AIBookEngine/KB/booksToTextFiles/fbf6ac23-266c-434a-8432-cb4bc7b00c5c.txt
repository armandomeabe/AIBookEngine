From the well-known Aesop's fable. Sam is lonely and bored, watching the sheep on the mountain. One day he makes up a story: "Come quickly! A wolf is attacking the sheep!" The villagers come running, but of course there's no wolf. Then, when a wolf really does attack, who will believe Sam?
