Move   Angela Holman, Barbara Webb, Bill Bowler, Peter Maggs, Rebecca Robb Benne,  Sue Kay, Sue Parminter   A1 to C1   Elementary to Pre-intermediate  A uniquely flexible course for adults and young adults. Move has a  carefully-graded communicative syllabus that helps students to express  themselves through English. It is ideal for short intensive courses or long  courses with fewer teaching hours.  Online Technical support is now available for the Move CD-ROMs.      KEY FEATURES    A Students's Book    Organised into three self-contained modules   Each module deals with a set of related topics under a thematic heading and  provides 15 hours of class work.   An interactive CD-ROM is included with every coursebook and provides extra  self-study practice.   Engaging topics motivate students to respond personally.
