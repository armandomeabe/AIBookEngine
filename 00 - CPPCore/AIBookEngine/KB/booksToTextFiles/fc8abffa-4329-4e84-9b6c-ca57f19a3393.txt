Four authentic practice tests for the Cambridge English: Preliminary (PET) exam from Cambridge English Language Assessment. The Student's Book without answers is perfect for classroom-based test practice.Audio CDs containing the recordings for the Listening test are available separately or as part of the self-study edition (containing the Student's Book with answers and Audio CDs), also available separately.
