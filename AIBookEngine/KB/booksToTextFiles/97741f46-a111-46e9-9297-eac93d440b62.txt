THE LITTLE MERMAID - EARLYREADS 3 (PRE-A1)The little mermaid lives in a magical, beautiful world at the bottom of the  sea, but she wants to see what the world above is like. When she goes up she  falls in love with a human. What will the little mermaid have to do to be  able to have a life in the world above with the man she loves?Adapted by Judith PercivalLanguage ENGLISHGenre Fairy tale & FantasyLevel Pre-level A1Examinations Starters
