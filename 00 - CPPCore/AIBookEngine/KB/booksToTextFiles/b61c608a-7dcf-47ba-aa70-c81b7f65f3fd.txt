Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day  Whether reacting to a day that goes from bad to worse, or to the discovery  that he's about to be moved across country, Alexander's emotions repeatedly  get the best of him. These are amusing accounts of a little boy's encounters  with the everyday and not-so-everyday surprises in life.    When Alexander wakes up he finds bubble gum in his hair...and from there the  day only gets worse. An amusing account of a little boy's encounter with  Murphy's law.
