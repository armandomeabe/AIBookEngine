AROUND THE WORLD IN EIGHTY DAYS - READING AND TRAINING 2 (B1.1) In 1872 a rich English gentleman, Phileas Fogg, makes a bet that he can go around the world in 80 days. So along with his French manservant, Passepartout, he sets off on an incredible race against the clock, over land and sea.Dossiers:Great Journeys around the WorldPassenger ships and Transatlantic TravelAuthor Jules VerneAdapted by Eleanor DonaldsonLanguage ENGLISHGenre AdventureLevel B1.1Examinations Preliminary, Trinity
