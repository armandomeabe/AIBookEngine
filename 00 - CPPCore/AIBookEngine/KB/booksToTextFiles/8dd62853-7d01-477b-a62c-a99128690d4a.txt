BUGABOO THE WICKED WITCH - EARLYREADS 3 (PRE-A1)This is a story about a witch who is so stupid that she is actually  harmless! She becomes extremely dangerous however, when a blow to her head  turns her into a good witch. There is nothing worse than a very stupid  person who wants to do good, particularly if she has magic powers on her  side!Author Cristina IvaldiLanguage ENGLISHGenre Fairy tale & FantasyLevel Pre-level A1Examinations Starters
