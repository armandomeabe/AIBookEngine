Cambridge Key English Test 1 is the first collection of practice tests  published by Cambridge University Press for this level.    This title has been thoroughly updated to conform to the latest  specifications for the revised examination (to be introduced in March 2004).      It contains:  • a helpful overview of the Ket exam   • four exam papers fro Cambridge ESOL   • attractive visual material to stimulate discussion and help you practise  for the paired Speaking test (paper 3)   • photocopiable answer sheets so you can practice transferring your answers    • The 'with answers' edition contains a full answer key and tapescripts of  the recorded material.
