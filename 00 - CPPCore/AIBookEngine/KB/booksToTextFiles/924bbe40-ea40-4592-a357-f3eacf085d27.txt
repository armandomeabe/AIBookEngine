CEF A2 / B1 Waystage /  Threshold  Cambridge ESOL PET  Trinity Levels 4,5      Operation Osprey, by David A. Hill    Don and Mike are best friends. They both live in the sleepy town of Saltley  and they both love birdwatching.  Their lives suddenly become exciting when Mike spots a pair of osprey at a  nearby lake. The boys decide to protect the birds so that they can make a  nest. But when Mr Roberts takes an interest in the birds the boys become  suspicious.
