ANNA KARENINA    Macmillan Readers   Various   The Macmillan Readers series is perhaps one of the world's most popular and  respected series of simplified readers worldwide. Product Description  Tells the story of Anna's forbidden love for the dashing soldier, Count  Vronsky. This work is a study of passionate love set against the background  of pre-Bolshevik Russia at the time of the development of the Russian  railway.
