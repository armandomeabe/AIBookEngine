A 6-level child-centred course for Primary teachers.All about real lifeStudents share the experiences of everyday life alongside a real English-speaking schoolchild, activating their curiosity and making learning memorable.All about working togetherCollaborative unit projects get students working together, encouraging them to discuss their own opinions and learn from one another.All about engaging every childUnprecedented video content engages all learners, and inclusive projects, extension and revision activities, plus two level editable tests help every child reach their potential.All about thinkingReal-life stories come from many different genres, encouraging students to reflect on values in a communicative context.
