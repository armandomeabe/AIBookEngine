LONDON - READING & TRAINING 1 (A2) DISCOVERYHow many different languages are spoken in London? What's the Square Mile all about? How did Piccadilly get its name? Book a seat in a haunted theatre and enjoy the show! Get to know this sensational city starting from its glorious history all the way to its captivating present. Discover the magic that is London!Dossiers:London and its WritersLondon's Great Parks and MuseumsAuthor	Gina D.B. ClemenLanguage	ENGLISHGenre	Non fictionLevel	A2Examinations	Key, Trinity
