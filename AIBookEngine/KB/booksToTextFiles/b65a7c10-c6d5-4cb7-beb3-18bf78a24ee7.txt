THE PERFECT FIT -     9780192774613A story about happiness, differences and shapes!Author Naomi Jones and Illustrator James JonesSuitable for:  2+ A story everyone can relate to about trying to fit inTriangle is trying her best to fit in, but she's just not like the circles and the squares.
