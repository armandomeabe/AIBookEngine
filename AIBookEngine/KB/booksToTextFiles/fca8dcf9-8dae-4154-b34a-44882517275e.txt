face2face Second edition is the flexible, easy-to-teach, 6-level course (A1  to C1) for busy teachers who want to get their adult and young adult  learners to communicate with confidence. face2face is informed by Cambridge  English Corpus and the English Vocabulary Profile, meaning students learn  the language they really need at each CEFR level.    The Starter Workbook without Key is ideal for setting homework tasks to  consolidate the learning in the Student's Book. The Workbook offers  additional consolidation activities and a Reading and Writing Portfolio for  extra skills practice, and a list of 'Can do' statements helps students to  track their own progress in reading and writing.
