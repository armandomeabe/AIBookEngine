Cookie and Friends   Vanessa Reilly and Kathryn Harper       Cookie and Friends is a three-level, story-based course with a rich package  of resources that are practical and fun - perfect for the very young.    Part of: Cookie and Friends   Other levels: Starter , B   Level: Pre-school     Cookie and Friends offers you everything you need to teach 3-6 year olds.    The incredible resources, including posters, flashcards, a puppet, CD-ROM  and Audio CD are not only fun for your students, but help you to establish  classroom routines.    The delightful stories are brought to life through the beautiful A4 story  cards.    Plus - the tear-out worksheets develop pre-reading and pre-writing skills as  well as language learning.
