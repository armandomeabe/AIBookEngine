The Silver Chair (rack)  By C. S. Lewis   Illustrated by Pauline Baynes        Narnia ... where giants wreak havoc ... where evil weaves a spell ... where  enchantment rules.    Through dangers untold and caverns deep and dark, a noble band of friends  are sent to rescue a prince held captive. But their mission to Underland  brings them face-to-face with an evil more beautiful and more deadly than  they ever expected.
