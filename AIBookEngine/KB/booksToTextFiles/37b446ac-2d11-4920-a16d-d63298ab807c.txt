IELTS Language Practice is aimed at all students preparing to sit their  IELTS tests. It retains many of the features that make this series so  popular - clear grammar explanations, themed vocabulary units to centralise  words and phrases and a variety of exercise types to provide plenty of  practice.    KEY FEATURES   Grammar and vocabulary units target key topic and language areas for  students preparing for the IELTS exam   Lexico-grammatical (words and phrases) units tackle areas of particular  difficulty for students
