Oxford Preparation & Practice for Cambridge English: First for Schools Exam Trainer Student's Book Pack with Key.Preparing students for the Cambridge English: First for Schools exam.An exam preparation title for teachers looking to prepare students for the Cambridge English B2 First for Schools exam in conjunction with a main course book. Through its combination of systematic training activities and practice tests, students will be well prepared and can sit the exam with confidence.This Student's Book Pack contains all the resources your students need to achieve success in the Cambridge English B2 First for Schools exam, including seven practice tests (six print tests and one online), a speaking DVD-ROM, two audio CDs, and a key containing explanatory answer keys and model writing answers.
