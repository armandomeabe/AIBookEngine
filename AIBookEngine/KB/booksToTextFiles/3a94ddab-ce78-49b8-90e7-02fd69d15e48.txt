Cambridge BEC 4 Higher Student's Book with answers  Examination Papers from University of Cambridge ESOL Examinations    Cambridge BEC 4 Higher provides a set of four complete practice tests for  the Cambridge BEC Higher exam. These past examination papers from University  of Cambridge ESOL Examinations (Cambridge ESOL) provide the most authentic  exam preparation available. They allow candidates to familiarise themselves  with the content and format of the examination and to practise useful  examination techniques. The Student's Book also contains a comprehensive  section of keys and transcripts, making it suitable for self study.
