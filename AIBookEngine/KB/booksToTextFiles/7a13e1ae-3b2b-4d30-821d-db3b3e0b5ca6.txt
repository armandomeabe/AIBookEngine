On the Pulse 2nd Edition is a dynamic 4-level series for teenage learners in the 21st century. The updated topics and texts, the multiple opportunities for reflection on language and cultural comparison, the systematic recycling and integration, and a new design that is both modern and clear guarantee learning for all students!The inclusion of new and updated texts relevant to teenagers helps teachers to keep students interested and motivated.The new Extra Grammar Practice section and the Skills Builder make the book more flexible and adaptable to multi-level classes.Students' digital competences are fostered through: a new Project per unit, a new Student App with interactive practice and suggested video searches for the Pulse Magazine pages on the eBook.A SEN booklet is provided in the Teacher's Resource Centre with tips, activity accommodations and test adaptations for students with special education needs.
