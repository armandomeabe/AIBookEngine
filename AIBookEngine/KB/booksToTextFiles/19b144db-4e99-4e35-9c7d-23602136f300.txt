Big English British is the most complete package for young learners. It  gives your students all they need to learn English. And it gives you  everything you need to engage your students and get great results.     Activities present key language in context, and include songs, stories,  sticker activities, content language and values lessons to help students  develop 21st Century Skills. Regular four-page checkpoints focus on  Assessment for Learning and allow students to assess their own progress.  Each book includes a Young Learner's Exams preparation section.
