THE PROBLEM OF CELL 13 - READING & TRAINING 5 (B2.2)'Lock me in any cell in any prison anywhere at any time, wearing only normal clothes and I'll escape in a week.' For Professor Van Dusen, otherwise known as 'The Thinking Machine', nothing is impossible. Logic is his passion. One hour later he finds himself locked up in Chisholm prison and has to use all his mental resources to find the solution to the problem.Dossiers:Other Prison StoriesFutrelle's Death on the TitanicAuthor	Jacques FutrelleAdapted by	Graeme Thomson, Silvia ThomsonLanguage	ENGLISHGenre	Mystery & HorrorLevel	B2.2
