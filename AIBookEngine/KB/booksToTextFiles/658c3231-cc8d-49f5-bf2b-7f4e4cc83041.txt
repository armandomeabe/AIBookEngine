Curso PONS. SpanishComplete self-study courseComprehensive self-study course of Spanish based on the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages. The course consists of 2 books (1 course book + 1 reference book) and 4 CDs and is addressed both to beginners and false beginners.The Course book includes 16 lessons and 4 review chapters. In the Reference book you will find the transcriptions of the dialogues and solutions to the exercises, a summary of the grammar introduced during the course, and a bilingual Spanish-English glossary. The course comes together with 4 CDs, with which you will be able to practise the listening skills.
