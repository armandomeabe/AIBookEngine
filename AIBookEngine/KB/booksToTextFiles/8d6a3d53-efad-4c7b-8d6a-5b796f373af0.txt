Put your chemistry to the test when you take over a chocolate factory. Get to grips with biology as you venture through the Amazon Rainforest. Swat up on astronomy as you navigate through the Solar System. And use your physics know-how to escape a haunted fun fair!Readers must use their problem-solving skills and scientific knowledge to navigate through four thrilling adventures. Science Quest follows the popular Maths Quest series. Questions are carefully chosen to address core science subjects for the age group. Finding the answers enables readers to advance through the story, learning more about science with every step they take.Clues are dotted along the way, and wrong turns will direct readers towards the right answer! A glossary explains scientific words and provides added reference material.
