A focused, 50-60 hour course for the revised Cambridge English: First (FCE)  for Schools exam from 2015. The Teacher's Book supports teachers with lesson  preparation ideas and features CLIL extension tasks. It contains full answer  keys, with model answers for the writing tasks, and audioscripts for both  the Student's Book and Workbook. Photocopiable Progress tests are also  included.    In addition, two full practice tests are available online for teachers to  access.
