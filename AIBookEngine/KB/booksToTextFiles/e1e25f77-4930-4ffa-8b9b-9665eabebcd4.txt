A new addition to the Complete English as a Second Language series, this Exam Success Guide provides the tools essential for achievement at IGCSE assessment. Featuring four practice papers, study tips and strategies for self-assessment, the Guide fully supports the new Cambridge syllabus, for first examination in 2019.FeaturesBuild confidence with practice papers, in the style of the latest syllabus, for first examination in 2019.Thoroughly familiarize learners with assessment requirements, expectations and style, using exam-style reading, writing, listening and speaking questions.Boost performance with study tips from examiners.Encourage independent learning with suggestions for self-assessment strategies.
