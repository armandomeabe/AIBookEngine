Little ones will love saying 'hello' to a whole host of friendly woodland creatures in this delightful lift-the-flap book. Sam Boughton's vibrant illustrations bring facts to life with deft humour and memorable comparisons. Introduces the youngest readers to favourite animals with gentle facts, friendly facts and simple comparisons. It includes all of these animals: deer, fox, rabbit, bear, beaver, mouse, owl, wolf, squirrel and bat. With big flaps to lift and a fantastic double gatefold on the final spread, this book is sure to go down a storm with young readers.
