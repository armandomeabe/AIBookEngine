Harold's Christmastime adventure, in print again for the first time in years!It's Christmas Eve, and Harold needs a Christmas tree. Purple crayon in hand, he sets out in search of one and soon finds himself at the North Pole, where a snowbound (and very purple) Santa Claus needs his help!This classic character's holiday adventure will delight fans young and old, and makes a charming Christmas gift. Its small size makes it a perfect stocking stuffer, too!
