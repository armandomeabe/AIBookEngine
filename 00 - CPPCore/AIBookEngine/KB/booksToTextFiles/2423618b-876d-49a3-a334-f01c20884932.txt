Who puts out fires? What do zookeepers do? Where does an astronaut work? Macmillan Factual Readers are a six-level series allowing learners of English to explore a variety of fascinating real-world topics. Te controlled use of vocabulary and structures make them the perfect CLIL Readers.Carefully graded and including a glossary with explanations of key vocabulary, Macmillan Factual Readers are aimed at 6- to 12-year-olds in need of more challenging reading material. This is indicated by the + sign next to each level.
