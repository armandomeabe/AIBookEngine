Elementary - Intermediate. For Teenagers and Adults.  The Longman Diccionario Pocket is designed for secondary students in Latin  America. It is based on a corpus of English, of course, but it also uses a  Latin American Spanish corpus especially created for this dictionary, with  written and students want English equivalents are words that they use  themselves, and the Spanish equivalents for English words are words they are  familiar with.     All the explanations in the Longman Diccionario Pocket are in Spanish, and  the examples reflect the world that the students live in.
