Authentic examination papers for learners preparing for the revised Cambridge English: Young Learners from 2018. This Answer Booklet accompanies Cambridge English: Starters 1 Student's Book and Audio CD. It contains answers for all three tests, the tapescripts and examples of the type of interchange to expect in the Speaking test. It also includes the Cambridge English: Starters Vocabulary list, organised by theme. An Audio CD (which contains the listening sections of the tests) and the Student's Book are available separately.
