CHARLIE AND THE BIRTHDAY PARTY - HELLO KIDS READERS STARTER      Charlie and the Birthday Party is a children's book about a young boy named Charlie who is invited to his friend's birthday party. The book is written in English and is suitable for children in their second year of primary education.The story begins with Charlie receiving an invitation to his friend Max's birthday party. Charlie is excited and can't wait to go to the party. He decides to make a birthday card for Max as a present.Nivel educativo: Educación primariaCurso: Segundo de PrimariaMateria :InglésMCE:A1Autores:S. Finnie, D. Bourdais, Y. Robert
