CLICK, CLACK, MOO - COWS THAT TYPEFarmer Brown has a problem. His cows like to type. All day long he hears Click, clack, MOO.Click, clack, MOO.Clickety, clack, MOO.Farmer Brown thinks it's odd when he hears typing sounds coming from the barn. But his troubles really begin when his cows start leaving him notes. First they demand better working conditions, then they stage a strike.Doreen Cronin's understated text and Betsy Lewin's expressive illustrations make the most of this hilarious situation.
