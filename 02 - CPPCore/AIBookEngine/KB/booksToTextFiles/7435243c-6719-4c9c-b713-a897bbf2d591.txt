Discover finger-tracing fun as you trace the trails and follow the dinosaur through the busy prehistoric pictures!Explore the colourful illustrations as you trace the grooved trails on each page with your fingers and follow the dinosaur through the five different prehistoric scenes. Each scene is full of lots of other stomping, chomping and roaring dinosaurs to spot too. There are die-cuts to find and feel, plus holes lead you through the pages as you follow that dinosaur and find out where he is going!The tactile trails and die-cut holes are perfect for fun sensory and interactive play. Bright colourful illustrations feature lots of detail to spot and say and aid development of first words and early reading.
