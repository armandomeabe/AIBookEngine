Take the hard work out of lesson preparation with this unique collection of  photocopiable activities. Written by an experienced ESOL teacher, for ESOL  teachers this photocopiable resource book is ideal for UK Skills for Life  exams, ESOL for Work exams, Government Citizenship tests, and General ESOL  courses.    Each of the activities is free-standing, and comprehensive teacher's notes  give a clear indication of any preparation required, as well as suggestions  for differentiation. There are also self-study exercises linked to every  activity, which are perfect for use as ready-made homework tasks.    This book is suitable for Entry Level 2 students.
