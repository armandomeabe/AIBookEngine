Make and Do: Pirates welcomes young adventurers to a swashbuckling world of pirate fun. Lively, informative text and bright, stylish designs by renowned children's illustrator Marion Billet encourage counting and word association in the liveliest possible ways. Fun activities on every page provide hours of absorbing play; make Captain Koala and his treasure chest from press-out card pieces, stickers, complete the dot-to-dots, follow the mazes and trails or learn how to draw the pirate friends! Make and Do: Pirates is packed with creative fun for young pirates everywhere.
