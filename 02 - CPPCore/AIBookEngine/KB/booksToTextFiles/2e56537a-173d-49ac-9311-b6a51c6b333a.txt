Here comes Mighty Small! He may be the smallest superhero in the town, but he's also the bravest, too. So when the circus clowns pinch all the gold from the town's bank, there's only one thing he can do . . . Max leaps into action shouting, "Baddie pants, beware!" and he tracks the clowns all the way back to their Big Top hide-out, where their boss, Mr Big, is waiting to fill his trunk with gold. Will Max be able to overthrow Mr Big and return the gold to the town? Find out in this action-packed superhero adventure that will have you cheering for the underdog!
