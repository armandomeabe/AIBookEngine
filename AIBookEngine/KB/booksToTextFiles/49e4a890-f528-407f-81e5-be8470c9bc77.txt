For twenty years, Zorro has beenCalifornia's worst prison. When RafaelMontero put him there, Zorro lost hisfamily. He felt that his life was over. WhenMontero comes back to California, thepeople need Zorro again. Zorro wants tohelp but he's an old man now. Can he finda new Zorro?Can he stop Montero'splans? And can he win back the love of hisbeautiful daughter, Elena?
