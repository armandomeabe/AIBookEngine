Yippee New Blue - is an innovative three-level beginners course in British  and American English. Designed for very young learners, the Yippee! course,  which consists of the Red Book, the Green Book and the Blue Book, is sure to  thrill and delight children. It is based on the Multiple Intelligences  Theory and its aim is to help children learn English the same way that  native speakers do - by listening and speaking first, before they learn to  read and write.
