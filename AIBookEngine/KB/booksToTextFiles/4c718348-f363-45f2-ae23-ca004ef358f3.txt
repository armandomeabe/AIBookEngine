Beautiful world photography, captivating real-life video and interest-grabbing CLIL topics take young learners on a remarkable journey to explore the world as they learn English. The full-colour Guess What! Teacher's Book Level 1 is interleaved with the Pupil's Book and offers pathways to help adapt the material to individual timetables. It contains: Step by step guidance for each lesson, including a summary of lesson aims and materials needed, simple warmers and end of lesson activities, audio scripts and answer keys. Photocopiable word cards for each unit to match the flashcards. Extra games and activities for every lesson, as well as a Games bank can be used as a dip-in resource. A DVD with the captivating videos for the CLIL lessons.
