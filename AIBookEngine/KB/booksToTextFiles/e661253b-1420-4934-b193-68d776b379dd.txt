JULIUS CAESAR - READING & TRAINING 3 (B1.2)There is discontent in Rome among the people and aristocrats. The people are angry because of a grain shortage and the aristocrats are worried that Julius Caesar will seek to become king. A group of conspirators led by Brutus decides to assassinate Caesar. At first everything seems to go well for them, but soon the conspirators find themselves pursued by the 'Spirit of Caesar.Shakespeares text has been adapted into an accessible narrated story which retains the drama of the original play.Author William ShakespeareAdapted by James ButlerActivities by Adeline RichardsLanguage ENGLISHGenre TragedyLevel B1.2Examinations Preliminary, Trinity
