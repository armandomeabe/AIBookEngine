Achievers combines interesting, age-appropriate topics with challenging input, practice and support so that every student will achieve his or her best.Achievers covers CEFR levels A1+ to C1 and includes exam training sections to help students prepare for TOEFL and Cambridge exams.With Achievers students will:learn contemporary, colloquial English. improve their vocabulary through lexical sets and vocabulary-building sections.  focus on Cambridge and TOEFL exams through an exam training section. partake in an ambitious projectlike speaking task to develop their speaking skills.
