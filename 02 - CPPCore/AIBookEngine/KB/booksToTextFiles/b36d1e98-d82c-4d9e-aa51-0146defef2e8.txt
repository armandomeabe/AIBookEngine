GIVE ME FIVE 5 - WORKBOOK + DIGITAL N/ED (2023)Give Me Five! is an engaging 6-level primary course with a strong focus on collaboration and 21st century skills. The dynamic and fun content keeps students motivated to reach learning goals and achieve success in external exams.Designed to engage and build students' confidence as they learn to communicate, Give Me Five! provides unique opportunities for both free and controlled speaking practice across a range of contexts. Participation, learner autonomy and social competency all take centre stage in order to help young learners reach their true potential.This blended solution of the print Activity Book and Digital Activity Book enables students to have extra practice while studying face-to-face or online.
