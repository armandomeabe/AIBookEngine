Fun, cultural and informative, this series of readers offers an extensive range of fiction and non-fiction titles that reinforce the basic structures and vocabulary found in most primary courses. Children will love the exciting adventures in stories such as Lights, Camera, Action, A Hungry Visitor and A Thief in the Museum. The Macmillan Children's Readers series can be used as supplementary reading material with any primary course. Key Features A picture dictionary (or word list) and activity pages at the back of each reader Accompanying Audio (on CD or on the website www.macmillanenglish.com) gives children a chance to listen to the stories while they read them The blend of Fact Files and topical fictional stories in levels 3-6 motivates older children and appeals to boys and girls equally
