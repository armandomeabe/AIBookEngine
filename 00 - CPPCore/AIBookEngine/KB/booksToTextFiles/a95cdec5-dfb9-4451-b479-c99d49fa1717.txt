My Fist English Adventure offers 3 to 5 year-olds a stimulating first  contact with English while:     Providing a motivating context  Fostering a positive attitude towards the new language  Involving them emotionally through well-known stories, friendly, characters  and age-appropiate activities  My First English Adventure offers teachers all they need to achieve high  goals:    Lesson-by-lesson full-colour teaching notes   Methodology and working suggestions   Explanations for the use of all components   Opportunities to exploit Disney films   Progress assessment activities
