FREDDY FINDS THE THIEF - EARLYREADS 4 (EARLY A1)Freddy Finds the Thief gives children their first taste of English in the  form of a play: they read and act out the adventures of Freddy, who helps  the king to unveil a thief in the castle. They will meet an assortment of  lively characters who add colour to the story and personality to the script.  In addition, the exercises offer the opportunity to 'play' with words,  listen to and act out a series of amusing sketches and above all, lose their  inhibitions and develop fluency when speaking English.Author Victoria HewardLanguage ENGLISHGenre AdventureLevel Early A1Examinations Movers
