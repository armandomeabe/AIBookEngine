BEBOP AND FRIENDS 2 - WORKBOOK + ACTIVITY EBOOKBebop and Friends is a 3-level pre-primary course which brings together music, play and well-placed language syllabus to help teachers ensure a positive start to learning English for their youngest learners.Building on the trusted and loved methodology of the original edition this course brings in new characters, a focus on global values, and new components to make learning even more memorable and enjoyable.
