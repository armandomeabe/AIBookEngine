Chaverim B'Ivrit was developed for the liberal day-school environment and includes selections from Jewish source texts and the siddur. This new approach to teaching Hebrew is based on the most current understanding of language acquisition in children, including the need to transfer and apply learning in new contexts and situations. These full-color, field tested booklets treat Hebrew as a living language. Teacher's guidelines are at the beginning of each book, as well as at the bottom of most pages. This is Volume 1 of the series
