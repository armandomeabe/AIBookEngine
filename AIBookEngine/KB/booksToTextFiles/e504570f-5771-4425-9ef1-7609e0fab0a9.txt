A 3-level intensive pre-primary course (GSE 10-25, CEFR Pre A1-A1), introducing students to pre-coding, mathematical thinking, creative thinking, socio-emotional learning and literacy through hands-on play.With a Journey Program (3-5 hours a week) and Immersion Program (6-10 hours a week).CBeebies videos provide authentic content from the BBC's young children channel, and introduce the theme of each unit with opportunities for real communication in English.Big Questions provided as unit titles give meaningful context for conversation and language tasks.Pre-school learners take their first steps in discovering STEAM subjects through experiential learning that guides them to find answers through investigating, experimenting, and building new skills and understanding of the world around them.
