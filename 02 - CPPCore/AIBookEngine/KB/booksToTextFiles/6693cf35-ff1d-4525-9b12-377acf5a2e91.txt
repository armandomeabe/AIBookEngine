Basic Vocabulary in Use is a reference and practice book for students of  English at the basic level. It can be used in the classroom or for homework.    • teaches approximately 1,200 useful words and expressions  • contains 60 two-page units that cover different grammar- or content-based  topics  • gives vocabulary explanations and practice exercises on facing pages  • teaches words and phrases used for work, travel, weather, feelings,  greetings, animals, food and drink, and more  • includes a complete index with phonetic transcriptions  • includes an audio CD with recordings of carefully selected example  sentences from the book, so you can hear how to pronounce the words you  learn
