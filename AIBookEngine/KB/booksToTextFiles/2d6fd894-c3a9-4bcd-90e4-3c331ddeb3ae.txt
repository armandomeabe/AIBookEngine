The Just Right course is a five-level general English course for adults and  young adults.   Students learn in different ways. Just Right seamlessly integrates different  approaches, motivating students and encouraging them to learn rapidly.    Just Right's learner-centred approach produces confident learners able to  communicate freely and spontaneously outside the classroom.    Just Right is packed full of fresh, engaging material. Students will enjoy  using this book and teachers will find it flexible and easy to use.
