PUPS SAVE A SEAL - PAW PATROL - READING STARS 3Ryder and Cap'n Turbot take a boat trip. When they see a seal in trouble, they need the PAW Patrol's help!This engaging series of readers features popular characters from the Nickelodeon television series PAW Patrol, Dora the Explorer, Blaze and the Monster Machines and Team Umizoomi and will support children's first steps to reading success.
