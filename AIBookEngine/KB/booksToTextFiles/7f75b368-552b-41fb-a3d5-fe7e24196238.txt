Complete First for Schools is official preparation for the revised 2015  Cambridge English: First (FCE) for Schools exam. The course combines the  very best in contemporary classroom practice with engaging topics aimed at  younger students. The information, practice and advice contained in the  course ensure that they are fully prepared for all parts of the test, with  strategies and skills to maximise their score.    The Workbook provides extra practice of the language and skills covered in  the Complete First for Schools Student's Book. The Audio CD contains the  recordings for the Workbook listening exercises.    A Workbook with answers with Audio CD is also available, separately.
