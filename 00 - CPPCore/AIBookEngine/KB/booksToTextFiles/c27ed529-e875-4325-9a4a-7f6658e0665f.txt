Pop Out Stencil Art: Safari Animals shows you how to make an elephant puppet, a greeting card, chameleon bunting, minimenageries and much more. The stencil sets feature many different animals including a giraffe, a snake, an elephant, a lion and even a toucan! Pop-Out Stencil Art is the perfect way to show a young child how many ways there are to be creative. Featuring 15 easy to use pop-out stencils and 20 fun projects to make, these books will be sure to keep hands busy for hours. Each spread has three different sizes of pop-out stencils and creative ways to use them. The stencils can be reused time and again, and stored back in their nested shapes inside the book.
