THE NO.1 LADIE'S DETECTIVE AGENCY - PEARSON READERS 3Contemporary / British English Precious Ramotswe is a kind, warm-hearted and large African lady. She is also the only female private detective in Botswana. Her agency -- the No.1 Ladies' Detective Agency -- is the best in the country. With help of her secretary, Mma Makutsi, and her best friend, Mr JLB Matekoni, she solves a number of difficult problems. A missing husband, a missing finger and a missing child -- she will solve these mysteries in her own special way.
