Discover English - The journey begins.  Texts and dialogues present new language in a meaningful context relevant to  the students lives   Cultural sections encourage students to reflect on the differences between  other culture and their own   Fun Zone exercises including songs, raps, puzzles and games provide  light-hearted grammar, vocabulary, listening and speaking practice   Cartoon stories and hidden codes add a truly fun aspect whilst improving  students' processing skills   Let's Revise pages encourage students to actively recycle what they have  learnt giving them a sense of progress   Comprehensive teacher support for dealing with large group mixed ability  classes, classroom discipline and specific learning difficulties   CYLETS-style tasks provide natural practice of exam type activities
