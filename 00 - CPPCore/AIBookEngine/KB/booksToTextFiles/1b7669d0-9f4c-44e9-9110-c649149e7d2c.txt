Link It! is a six-level course connecting students with the grammar, vocabulary, and skills they need to communicate with confidence and succeed in English. The Student Pack A includes the Student Book, Workbook, and Practice Kit, and covers units 1 to 4. The Student Book builds all four skills, grammar, and vocabulary as well as communication competences with 'Let's talk about...' lessons and videos that model real English. The Workbook provides plenty of extra practice of the unit vocabulary, grammar and communication, as well as overall revision and exam practice.
