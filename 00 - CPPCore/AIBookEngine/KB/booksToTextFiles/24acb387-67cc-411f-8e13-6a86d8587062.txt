Delve into the wonderful world of Biff, Chip and Kipper and find out more about the characters, their friends and family. Discover how the children found the magic key, learn more about their exciting adventures and find answers to your children's questions, enjoying a variety of activities, puzzles and games along the way. Ideal for generating group discussions or for children to use independently, this is the perfect companion to the well-loved characters of the Biff,Chip and Kipper Stories, whether you're introducing them for the first time or indulging the fans.
