Allie Sheridan's world is falling apart. Her brother's run away from home. Her parents ignore her. And she's just been arrested. Again.This time her parents have had enough. They send Allie to Cimmeria Academy - far away from her London friends. The school is beautiful - filled with gorgeous, ultra-wealthy teens who travel by private jet, and were raised by nannies.Allie has never felt more of an outsider.Instead of hating boarding school though, Allie begins to feel happy. She's slowly making friends. And there's Carter, a brooding loner with whom she feels an instant connection. But when she is attacked late one night, the school suddenly begins to feel like a very dangerous place.Allie finds out that nothing at Cimmeria is what it seems.
