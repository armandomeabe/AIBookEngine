In this classic Jules Verne adventure story, a message that the passengers  and crew of the Duncan find in a shark's belly launches the search for the  missing Captain Grant. The passengers of the Duncan along with Captain  Grant's children set out on an exciting adventure which takes them from the  peaks of the Andes in South America into the hands of blood-thirsty Maori  warriors in New Zealand. Join them on this wonderful journey of courage,  persistence and discovery and find out whether they will be able to locate  Captain Grant.
