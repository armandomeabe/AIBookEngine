Includes Phonics and Literacy Book and extra Online Teacher's Resources.Separate to the Student Book and Activity Book, this introduces students to sounds and letters to better prepares them for reading and writing.Listening activities allow students to hear the sounds they are learning and chants mean that they can practice saying them.Writing practice is provided through tracing, letter outline, and gap-fill activities.Online Teacher's Resources provide extra phonics material including audio, phonics presentations, and printable phonics flashcards.Tips and advice for teaching this strand of the course is provided in a Teacher's Guide which can also be downloaded.To download the Online Teacher's Resources for the Phonics and Literacy Pack, go to WEB and use the access code In the back of the Phonics and Literacy Book.
