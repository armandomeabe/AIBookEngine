LE MORTE D'ARTHUR - READING & TRAINING 4 (B2.1)Le Morte d'Arthur tells the story of King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table. The tale begins with Arthur's birth, his education, and his rise to the throne. It also recounts the tragic love story of Sir Lancelot and Guinevere, the destruction of the Round Table and Arthur's mysterious disappearance or death.Dossiers:Medieval Romance LiteraturePlaces in the Arthurian StorySir Gawain and the Green KnightAuthor Sir Thomas MaloryAdapted by Victoria SpenceActivities by Kenneth BrodeyLanguage ENGLISHGenre LegendLevel B2.1Examinations First, Trinity
