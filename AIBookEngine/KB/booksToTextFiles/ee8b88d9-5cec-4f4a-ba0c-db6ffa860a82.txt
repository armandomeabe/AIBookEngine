Eight units of ten lessons provide core material which is then followed by ten pages of Cambridge English Qualification external exam material.Three 'Big Projects' per level enable students to develop collaboration and critical thinking skills.Each level has two pages of festival material which helps student's activate new language through real-world concepts.Three extended texts per level provide ample reading opportunities for students to develop reading skills and put vocabulary into practice.Cambridge English Qualifications exam-style tasks ensure students are fully-prepared for exams.
