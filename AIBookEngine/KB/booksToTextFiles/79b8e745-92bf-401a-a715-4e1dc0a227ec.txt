Life Vision helps students gain the language and skills they need to work out where they want to go next, and how exam success will help them take advantage of the global opportunities that proficiency in English brings.With the Student Book with Online Practice, learners use the Student Book to participate-In class, and access Online Practice to continue learning outside of class. Ideal for students who need to use print books in the classroom.Life Vision includes an Assessment for Learning approach in the teaching notes, workbook review lessons and tests to enable teachers to give-Informal assessment and effective feedback, and to help learners to analyse and accelerate their progress.Professional Development support helps you get started with Life Vision. Watch video tutorials to set up your course, access focus and position papers for expert advice and bite sized guidance on key issues and take short professional development modules for an introduction to key methodologies for your course.All your digital course materials and resources are accessed via the Oxford English Hub - a launchpad for learning: www.oxfordenglishhub.com.
