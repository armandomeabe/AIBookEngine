Rapid progress for high-level classesAdvanced English in Use takes students to a B2 level and helps them prepare for exams such as KET and PET. The integrated skills approach develops productive fluency and competence in spoken English.Collaborative learning opportunitiesFrequent opportunities for pair and group work, project work and communicative tasks encourage collaborative learning as well as individual initiative.Language, Literature and CultureAdvanced English in Use presents language in a real world context and exposes students to a wide range of materials with CLIL sections, literature pages and a culture magazine at the back of the book. The variety of topics facilitates an emphasis on vocabulary acquisition.
