The English Ladder is a four-level course designed to help pupils take their  first steps in English. Join Freddie the frog for fun, adventure and lively  language learning through engaging stories, challenging songs, games, tongue  twisters, and communication activities.    Part of a four-level course, this Level 2 Activity Book features practice of  target language from Pupil's Book Level 2, activities to draw, colour and  write, Me! sections that encourage personalised language learning and a  Songs Audio CD, including karaoke versions.
