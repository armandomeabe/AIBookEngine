Children will love exploring the lively, magical world of Bridge Town with their new friends Poppy, Rowan and Fern. The changing seasons provide a backdrop to the adventures of a diverse cast of characters that capture children's imagination and attention. Includes Teacher's Guide, Classroom Presentation Tool and Class Audio CDs Teacher's Guide provides step-by-step guidance on all content in the Class Book, including an introductory description of methodology and concept behind the course. Classroom Presentation Tool allows you to run smooth heads-up lessons by projecting the Class Book with embedded audio and video. Classroom Presentation Tool can be used across all devices and is available online and offline.
