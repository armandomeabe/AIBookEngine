Amsterdam works its fairy-tale magic in many ways: via the gabled, Golden  Age buildings; glinting, boat-filled canals; and especially the cosy,  centuries-old brown cafes, where candles burn low and beers froth high. Add  in mega art museums and funky street markets, and it's easy to see why this  atmospheric city is one of Europe's most popular getaways.
