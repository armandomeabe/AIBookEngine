Sathya, Isuri and their friends live near Weligama, a town in Sri Lanka.Every morning they go to school, and every afternoon they jump on a tuktuk to go to the Human Solidarity Children Village. Join them in this adventure to discover the beauty of Sri Lanka, the food, the animals and their favourite hobbies. Follow them during the day and you will learn about their country and their lives. SyllabusFamily, food, animals, school, dance, musicGrammar and syllabusPresent simplePast simple (regular and irregular verbs)Will for futureBe going toComparative and superlative formsAdverbs of frequencyAdverbs of degreeIn this Reader you will find:Games and language activities | An audio recording of the story | A picture dictionary | Authentic photosTagsFriends | Food | Music | Culture.
