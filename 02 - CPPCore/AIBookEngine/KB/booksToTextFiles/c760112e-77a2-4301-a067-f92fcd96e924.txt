Improve language skills and strengthen written performance. Matched to the objectives of the Cambridge Secondary 1 English curriculum, this practical guide equips students of all abilities to feel confident in their use of English grammar and in their written work, supporting achievement in the Checkpoint Test and at Cambridge IGCSE(R) level. Strengthen language skills with activities that respond to all grammar, punctuation and spelling objectives from the Cambridge Secondary 1 syllabus. Prepare learners for the Checkpoint Test using a practical, grammar-focused approach. Support native English speakers' and EAL students' with differentiated activities, tailored to all ability levels.
