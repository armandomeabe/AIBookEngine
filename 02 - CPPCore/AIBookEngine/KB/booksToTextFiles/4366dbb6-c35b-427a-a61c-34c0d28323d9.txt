Learn English with Dora the Explorer is a 3-level, pre-primary course that guides students as they take their first steps into the world of English, together with the adventurous Dora the Explorer.Take your students on a language-learning journey with activities, songs, and videos that will build confidence and develop the skills needed for primary school.Teaching with Learn English with Dora the Explorer is fun and effortless with a flexible package of resources to support your classes.The Class Audio CDs contain all the audio material for the activities in the Student Book.
