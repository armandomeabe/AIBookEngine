RAPUNZEL - EARLYREADS 2 (PRE-A1)A witch takes Rapunzel away from her parents when she is a small child. When Rapunzel grows into a beautiful young woman the witch locks her in a tower in the forest! However, the story ends happily with Rapunzel reunited with her parents and then marrying a prince!Adapted by	Ruth HobartLanguage	ENGLISHGenre	Fairy tale & FantasyLevel	Pre-level A1Examinations	Starters
