The easy-to-use Mouse and Me! Plus Activity Book provides a wide range of practice activities to consolidate what is taught in the Student Book.It can be used to check children's learning in class, or can be taken home to complete with parents.Share the adventures of Daisy, Robin and their favorite toy, Mouse, as they dress up in different costumes and explore the world through imaginative play!
