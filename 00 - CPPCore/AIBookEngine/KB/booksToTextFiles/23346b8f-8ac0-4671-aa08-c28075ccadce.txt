IMAGINARIUM KINDER 2 - STUDENT'S BOOK 1 PRESCHOOLImaginarium is our new K-9 program centered around Literacy, Learning, and Life Skills that integrates excellent hard skills, Print Literacy and Digital Literacy Reading Program, alongside soft skills development of Communication, Collaboration, Creativity, Critical Thinking, Citizenship, and Character Skills.Imaginarium has all its materials available in both digital and print format. It can be taught 100% digitally or applied in a hybrid classroom so that the teacher can decide how best to give the class and assess and review students' progress.All interactive activities in the Student Books, including weekly assessments, are reported via a centralized dashboard that allows the teacher to store grades easily and give efficient personalized feedback throughout the school year.
