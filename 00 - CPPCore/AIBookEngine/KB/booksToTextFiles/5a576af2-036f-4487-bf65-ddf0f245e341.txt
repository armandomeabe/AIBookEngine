Link your world together with Link It!Every level develops your students' communication competences and provides integrated support for Cambridge exams. With its well-rounded approach, Link It! creates confident communicators ready to achieve success.ISBN: 978-0-19-482460-6Link It! is a six-level course connecting students with the grammar, vocabulary, and skills they need to communicate with confidence and succeed in English.The Student Pack includes the Student Book, Workbook, and Practice Kit. The Student Book builds all four skills, grammar, and vocabulary as well as developing communication competences and providing exam support and practice.The Workbook provides plenty of extra practice of the unit vocabulary, grammar and communication, as well as overall revision and exam practice.With Practice Kit, students have access to interactive activities, media-rich content, and the ability to track their progress.
