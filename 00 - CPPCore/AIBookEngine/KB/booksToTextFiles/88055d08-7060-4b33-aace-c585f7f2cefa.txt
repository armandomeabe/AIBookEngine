An innovative colouring and doodle book for budding artists. This book is filled with adorable pugs to colour and doodle. Use the bold outlines to guide you, and let your imagination run wild! As you progress through the book, there will be more and more room for your own drawings - helping to develop your artistic skills! There are over 100 colourful stickers to use in the book or wherever you want. A must-have for pug lovers!
