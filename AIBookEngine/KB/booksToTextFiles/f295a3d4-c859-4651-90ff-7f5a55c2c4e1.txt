Kate Toms Nursery Rhymes is a wonderful new collection of favourite nursery rhymes combined with Kate Toms' beautiful, hand-stitched illustrations. This combination provides a lively and fresh interpretation of well-known nursery rhymes brought to life with illustrations full of whimsy and humour. This delightful book includes: Baa Baa Black Sheep; Humpty Dumpty; Here We Go Round The Mulberry Bush; Mary, Mary, Quite Contrary; Row, Row, Row Your Boat; Hickory Dickory Dock, This Little Piggy Went to Market; Pat-A-Cake, Pat-A-Cake, Incy Wincy Spider and Three Blind Mice. This is the perfect collection for parents and children to share and enjoy together.
