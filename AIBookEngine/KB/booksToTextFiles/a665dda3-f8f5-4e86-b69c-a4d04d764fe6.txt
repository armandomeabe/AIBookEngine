CHARLIE AND THE GREAT FIRE OF LONDON - HELLO KIDS READERS 3Charlie and the Great Fire of London is a children's book written by S. Finnie, D. Bourdais, and Y. Robert. The story is set in 1666 during the Great Fire of London and follows the adventures of a young boy named Charlie. It also teaches important lessons about bravery, teamwork, and resilience in the face of adversity.Nivel educativo:Educación primariaCurso:Quinto de PrimariaMateria:InglésMCE:A2Autores:S.
