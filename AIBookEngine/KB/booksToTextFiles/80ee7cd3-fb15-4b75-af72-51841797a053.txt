GREAT EXPECTATIONS - GREEN APPLE 1 (A2)Pip, a poor young boy, receives amazing news. Someone has given him a vast  amount of money and wants to make him a gentleman. Who is his mysterious  benefactor? Is it Miss Havisham, the strange lady who lives in an old house  with the beautiful Estella? Life is full of surprises...Dossiers:Ladies and GetlemenTransportation to AustraliaPlayscriptAuthor Charles DickensAdapted by Derek SellenActivities by Joanna BurgessLanguage ENGLISHGenre Human interestLevel A2Examinations Key, Trinity
