THE BREATHTAKING NEW NOVEL FROM AWARD-WINNING AUTHOR KEVIN BROOKSJeet was raised by a pack of wild dogs. Recaptured and rehumanized, Jeet now lives with the last of his people in an ancient walled town in the vast expanse of the Deathlands, besieged by a much larger enemy clan. They are preparing for a final battle and it's Jeet's task to record the events.Jeet is struggling to come to terms with his half-human, half-dog identity. Can the impending conflict and his relationship with another rehumanized dogchild shed any light on what it takes to be a survivor?
