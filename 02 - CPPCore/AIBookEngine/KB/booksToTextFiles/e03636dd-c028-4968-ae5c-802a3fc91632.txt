Queen of Air and Darkness is a Shadowhunters novel.What if damnation is the price of true love? Innocent blood has been spilled on the steps of the Council Hall, the sacred stronghold of the Shadowhunters. Their society now teeters on the brink of civil war. What they find there is a secret that may tear the Shadow World asunder and open a dark path into a future they could never have imagined.
