Together Again Beginning readers will welcome the return of several favorite  characters. Amber Brown stars in two titles by Paula Danziger, illus. by  Tony Ross. In It's a Fair Day, Amber Brown, the heroine and her best friend,  Justin, enjoy a vacation with their families. Unfortunately, things turn  sour when Amber's parents start arguing and she gets lost at the county  fair. Get Ready for Second Grade, Amber Brown focuses on first-day jitters  that are quickly quelled. (July)
