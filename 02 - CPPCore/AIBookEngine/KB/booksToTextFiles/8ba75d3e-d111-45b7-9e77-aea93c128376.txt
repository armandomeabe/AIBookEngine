Time for field trip fun on the farm! When Mrs. Bono announces a class field trip to a farm, no one is more excited than Wanda Doomsday. She has always wanted to visit a farm for a special, secret reason. While the rest of the class learns about pigs, roosters, and cows, Wanda spends her time searching for a most unusual farm animal. Will she find what's she's looking for at Flying Feathers Farm?
