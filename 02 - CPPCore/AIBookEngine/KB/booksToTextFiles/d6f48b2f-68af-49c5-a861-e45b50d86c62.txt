MORE! is a four-level course from a highly respected author team that's  bursting with features for lower secondary students. A Cyber Homework access  code on the CD-ROM gives students access to two sets of online interactive  exercises per unit. Teachers set and monitor the homework and receive  results for the whole class without doing any marking! Students and teacher  can send each other notes and questions via the online messaging system. For  more information visit www.cambridge.org/elt/more/cyberhomework A version of  the Student's Book with CD-ROM without Cyber Homework is also available.
