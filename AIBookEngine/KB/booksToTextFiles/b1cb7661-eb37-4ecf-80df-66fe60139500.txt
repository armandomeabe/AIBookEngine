BEAUTY AND THE BEATS - GREEN APPLE STARTER (A1)A rich man suddenly becomes poor and he goes to live in the country with his  children. One day he goes to the city on business. His daughters Rosalind  and Hortensia ask him for expensive presents, but his other daughter,  Beauty, only wants a rose.  On his way back the man takes a rose from the garden of a castle. But the  owner of the castle, a beast, becomes angry and demands something in  exchange.
