Mimi's Wheel.Mimi's Wheel is a 3-level course designed to give teachers all the tools they need to successfully manage groups of energetic pre-primary learners. With the engaging stories that pupils will instantly fall in love with, and the optimum level of support, teaching becomes both effective and enjoyable. Photographic flashcards are provided for every key vocabulary item in the course.Teacher's Book includes a bank of games and activities using flashcards.Flashcards can also be used together with the Wheel Mat for games and activites to presente and conslidate vocabulary.Digital version of flashcards is included in the teacher's resources bank available via Teacher's Navio App.
