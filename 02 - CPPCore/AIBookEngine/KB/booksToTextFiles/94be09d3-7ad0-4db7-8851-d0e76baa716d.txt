THE FISHERMAN AND HIS SOUL - READING & TRAINING 2 (B1.1)A story on love and friendship by Oscar Wilde. The story is about a  fisherman who catches a mermaid. He lets her go because she promises to help  him fish. Each day the mermaid appears and sings her song, so he falls in  love with her! However, she cannot marry him until he has lost his human  soul.Dossiers:Children's LiteratureAestheticismAuthor Oscar WildeAdapted by Kenneth BrodeyLanguage ENGLISHGenre Fairy tale & FantasyLevel B1.1Examinations Preliminary, Trinity
