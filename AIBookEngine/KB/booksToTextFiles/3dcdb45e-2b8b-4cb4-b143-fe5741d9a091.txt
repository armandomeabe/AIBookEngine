The innovative approach to topics related to children's everyday life stimulates their curiosity and inquisitive spirit, arousing their interest and encouraging their active participation in the learning process.Student's Book with fully integrated WorkbookStudent's Interactive activities with games, songs, karaoke version of songs and videosRichmond 3D Picture Dictionary AppTeacher's Book with the theoretical background of the series, suggestions for extra activities and detailed instructions for the development of the activities in the Student's BookAudio CD with listening activities, including comic strip stories, songs and karaoke versions of songsTeacher's Resource Material with tests, assessments and annual lesson planningDigital BookFlashcards.
