RECIPE FOR SUCCESS - READING & TRAINING 1 (A2)Seren wants to go on a school trip to Paris more than anything. But when her Dad won't even let her walk home from school by herself, how is she going to convince him? Determined not to give up on her dream, Seren comes up with a plan. But she quickly learns that even the best plans can go wrong, and even the biggest dreams can end up broken. She needs another plan fast. But will it be good enough to convince her Dad?Dossiers:A food journey across the United KingdomClaude MonetWalesAuthor	Andrea M.
