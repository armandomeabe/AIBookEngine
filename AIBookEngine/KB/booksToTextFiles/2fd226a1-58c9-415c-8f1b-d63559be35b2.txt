The past examination papers in Cambridge Key English Test 4 provide the most  authentic exam preparation available. They allow candidates to familiarize  themselves with the content and format of the examination and to practise  useful examination techniques. The Self-Study Pack contains the Student's  Book with Answers and the Audio CD. Student's Books are available in both  'with answers' and 'without answers' editions. Books are numbered to  indicate how many are available. Numbers do not refer to levels.
