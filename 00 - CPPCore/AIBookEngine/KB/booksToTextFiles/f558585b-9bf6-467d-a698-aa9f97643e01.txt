This second edition of Biology for the IB Diploma is updated for the new IB  syllabus for Biology, implemented in 2014. This series comprehensively  covers all aspects of the syllabus, including practical work, Theory of  Knowledge and the new Nature of Science content. The Coursebook offers  students an engaging introduction to the content as well as materials for  in-depth exam preparation. The new Exam Preparation Guide due in 2015 offers  further support to boost exam performance. Teachers can supplement their  teaching with the free resources on our IB teacher website, including  practical protocols, worksheets and lesson ideas.
