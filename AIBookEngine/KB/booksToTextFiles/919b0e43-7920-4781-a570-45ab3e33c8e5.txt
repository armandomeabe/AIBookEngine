Playtime B   Stories, DVD and play - start to learn real-life English the Playtime way!   Claire Selby       The fun, new three-level British English course with fully integrated DVD  motivating pre-schoolers to learn through memorable stories and songs.    Part of: Playtime   Other levels: Starter , A   Level: Beginner   Teaching hours: 67 (+30 optional)     This great new series focuses on the real-life pre-school environment, a  familiar setting for little ones to start learning English through other  subjects.     Monkey (the puppet) starts every lesson in a fun way and the children's  enthusiasm for English builds from the beginning.     Through easy-to-follow classroom routines for each lesson children also  learn to develop good habits and good behaviour.     Playtime's lively stories present natural language in familiar contexts -  using integrated DVD as well.
