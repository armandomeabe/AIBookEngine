Alfie and Sam are on a space mission to discover another planet, and they need your help! Find their equipment and learn how to steer the rocket, before encountering an alien and playing space basketball with his friends! Solve each puzzle on your way through fantastical settings and brilliant stories! You can trace through mazes with your finger and help each character and their faithful friends on their adventure. Puzzle Adventures combine an exciting adventure story with an important feature: the reader doesn't need to use a pen to complete the puzzles! Stories are based around characters that are popular for this age group: knights, astronauts, mermaids and unicorns. Different kinds of puzzles help to develop key skills of memory, visual recognition and deduction skills.
