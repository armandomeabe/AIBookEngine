THE RANSOM OF RED CHIEF - READING & TRAINING 2 (B1.1)The Ransom of Red Chief is a wonderfully funny and ironic story about two inexperienced kidnappers and their unusual 'victim', with a great surprise ending. O. Henry's unique talent enabled him to create a world full of ordinary people who did extraordinary things.Dossier:The Origins of the American PoliceAuthor	O. HenryAdapted by	Gina D.B.
