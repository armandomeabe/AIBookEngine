Selected and Edited by David Stuart Davies    The Best of Sherlock Holmes is a collection of twenty of the very best tales  from Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's fifty-six short stories featuring the  arch-sleuth. Basing his selection around the author's own twelve personal  favourites, David Stuart Davies has added a further eight sparkling stories  to Conan Doyle's 'Baker Street Dozen' creating a unique volume which distils  the pure essence of the world's most famous detective
