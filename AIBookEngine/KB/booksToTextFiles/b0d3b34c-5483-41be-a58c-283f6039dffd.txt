Pop-Out Stencil Art: Dinosaurs helps young children discover the many different ways to be creative using sturdy prehistoric animal/bird-shaped stencils. Pop-Out Stencil Art is the perfect way to show a young child how many ways there are to be creative. Featuring 15 easy to use pop-out stencils and 20 fun projects to make, these books will be sure to keep hands busy for hours. Each spread has three different sizes of pop-out wild animal stencils and three creative ways to use them. The stencils can be used time and again, and stored in their nested shapes inside the book.
