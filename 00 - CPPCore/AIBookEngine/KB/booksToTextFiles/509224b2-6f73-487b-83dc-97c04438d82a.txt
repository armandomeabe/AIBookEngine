AKRON HOUSE MYSTERY - GREEN APPLE 1 (A2)SYNOPSISCarol and Steve Taylor move into an old, mysterious house which hides many secrets. Carol hears footsteps at night, Steve finds an old love letter from the American Civil War and the people in town hear a woman crying in the night. Who is trying to give them a message and why?Carol, Steve and their friend Halim solve an incredibly difficult and touching mystery.Dossier:The American Civil WarThe Lewis and Clark ExpeditionThanksgiving	Author Gina D.B. ClemenLanguage ENGLISHGenre Mystery & HorrorLevel A2Examinations Key, Trinity
