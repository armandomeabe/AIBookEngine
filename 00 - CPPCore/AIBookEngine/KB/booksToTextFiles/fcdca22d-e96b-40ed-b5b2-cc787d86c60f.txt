Empowering words that help children to express their emotionsAuthor Helen Mortimer and Illustrator Cristina TrapaneseSuitable for:  3+ years, Reception and KS1, Y1-2-4, teachers, parents, librariansThis little hardback book creates a moment to focus on Being Healthy. It introduces words to talk about healthy bodies and minds with confidence and in a way that feels good. The engaging art style, fun characters and picture book feel make it perfect to share and it is packed with educational goodness to help children develop and grow.
