The English Ladder is a four-level course designed to help pupils take their  first steps in English. Join Freddie the frog for fun, adventure and lively  language learning through engaging stories, challenging songs, games, tongue  twisters, and communication activities.    This Level 2 Pupil's Book features creative stories introducing new  vocabulary, phonics activities for enjoyable pronunciation practice and a  CLIL feature in every unit, focusing on core subjects such as science and  maths.
