Pandy the Panda is a three-level English language course consisting of class  books, activity books and teacher's books designed to feature topics used in  kindergarten in L1 as the axis around which the course rotates. Young  learners will be captivated by the adventures of Pandy, his friends and  their playhouse.   It also motivates children through entertaining chants, songs, pen-to-paper  activities, games, puzzles, stickers, stories, etc.  Flexible course with a wide range of attractive, ageappropriate components  which will ensure enjoyable English  learning for very young learners.   Puppet, flashcards, posters, photo pages, chants, songs and personalisation  tasks to present and recycle vocabulary in a meaningful way   Focuses on key areas of child development while helping improve fi ne and  gross motor-skills as well as personal  awareness themes
