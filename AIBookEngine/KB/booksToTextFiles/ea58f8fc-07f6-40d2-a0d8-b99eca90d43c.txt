FAT TUESDAY    Burke Basile is a cop with nothing left to lose. Haunted by his partner's  death, his marriage and his career over, he focuses on his nemesis, Pinkie  Duvall, a flamboyant attorney who helps killers evade justice. Burke's  shocking revenge centers around kidnapping Remy, the lawyer's trophy wife.  But Burke hasn't planned on the electric attraction he'll feel for this  desperate woman, who rose from the slums of New Orleans to marry a man she  can never love. Nor can he predict the fierce duel that will explode as the  clock ticks toward midnight on Fat Tuesday, when all masks will be stripped  away--and Burke must confront his own terrifying secret.
