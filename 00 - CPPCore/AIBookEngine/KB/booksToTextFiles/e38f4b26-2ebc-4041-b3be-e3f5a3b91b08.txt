When Mr Cosmo the conjuror arrives in town with his wife and children,  everyone gets very excited. His colourful caravan has six pigeons flying  over it, six rabbits peeping out of it - but not one single person is  driving it! Some amazing magical treats are in store. Based on the classic  'Happy Families' card game, this highly entertaining series is ideal for  reading and sharing at home or at school. It is guided by the Education  Adviser, Brian Thompson, and written by the award-winning author, Allan  Ahlberg. 'The best thing to happen to beginner readers since Dr Seuss' -  Children's Rights Workshop.
