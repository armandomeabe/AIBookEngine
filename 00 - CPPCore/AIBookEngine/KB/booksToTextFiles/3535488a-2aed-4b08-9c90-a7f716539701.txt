AS LEVEL BUSINESS HANDBOOK    Andrew Gillespie has established a well-earned reputation as an examiner and  author in Business Studies. In this exciting new book he summarises a number  of A Level topics and engages the reader in a series of exercises designed  to test knowledge and increase exam skills. There is guidance on the precise  requirements of AQA, Edexcel and OCR, and each topic provides: * Questions  for self-testing with answers at the back * Examination questions and  answers for students to analyse * An analysis of how the author himself  would mark the answers .
