The Vampire Diaries: The Fury And Dark Reunion   Elena: transformed, the golden girl has become what she once feared and  desired.     Stefan: tormented by losing Elena, he's determined to end his feud with  Damon once and for all-whatever the cost. But slowly he begins to realize  that his brother is not his only enemy.
