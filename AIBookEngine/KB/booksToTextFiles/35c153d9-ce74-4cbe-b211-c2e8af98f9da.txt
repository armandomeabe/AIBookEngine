PERSPECTIVES INTERMEDIATE A - STUDENT'S BOOK SPLIT A + ONLINE PRACTICEPerspectives is a four-skills series in American and British English that teaches learners to think critically and to develop the language skills they need to find their own voice in English. The carefully-guided language lessons, real-world stories, and TED Talks motivate learners to think creatively and communicate effectively. In Perspectives, learners develop an open mind, a critical eye and a clear voice in English.
