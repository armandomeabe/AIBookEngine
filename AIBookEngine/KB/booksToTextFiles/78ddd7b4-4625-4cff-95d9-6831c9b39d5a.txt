L'italiano all'università 1 for English speakersMatteo La GrassaItalian course for universities and language institutes. Beginners-Elementary (A1-A2)L'italiano all'università 1 for English speakers is aimed primarily at university students who are studying Italian at university in Italy or abroad.The volume comprises the Student's book with 12 teaching units and the Workbook, with 12 units and keys to the exercises. At the end of each unit there is either an assessment test on the linguistic and communicative skills or a self evaluation table with the corresponding solutions.
