An innovative, new multi-level course for the university and in-company  sector. Business Advantage is the course for tomorrow's business leaders.  Based on a unique syllabus that combines current business theory, business  in practice and business skills - all presented using authentic, expert  input - the course contains specific business-related outcomes that make the  material highly relevant and engaging.    The Business Advantage Intermediate level books include input from the  following leading institutions and organisations: the Cambridge Judge  Business School, IKEA, Emirates NBD, Isuzu and Unilever - to name but a few.    The Audio CDs (2) contain all of the listening materials accompanying the  Student's Book.
