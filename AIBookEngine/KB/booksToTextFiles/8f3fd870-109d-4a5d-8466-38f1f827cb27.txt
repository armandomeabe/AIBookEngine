Macmillan Children's Readers: A variety of fiction and non-fiction titles in  six levels for children aged from 6 to 12 years old.    Fun, cultural and informative, this series of readers offers an extensive  range of fiction and non-fiction titles that reinforce the basic structures  and vocabulary found in most primary courses.    Children will love the exciting adventures in stories such as Hide and Seek,  A Hungry Visitor and A Thief in the Museum.
