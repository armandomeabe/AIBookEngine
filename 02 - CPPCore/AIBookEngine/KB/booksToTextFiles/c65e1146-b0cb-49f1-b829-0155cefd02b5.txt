English for International Tourism is designed to meet the English language needs of professionals working in the tourism industry and students of tourism in further education. The course includes authentic material taken from Dorling Kindersley's acclaimed Eyewitness Travel Guides which explore some of the world's top tourist destinations.The series builds learner confidence in the professional skills needed for the tourist industry whilst developing their language awareness. Students practise these skills in realistic Case Studies that reflect topical tourism issues.
