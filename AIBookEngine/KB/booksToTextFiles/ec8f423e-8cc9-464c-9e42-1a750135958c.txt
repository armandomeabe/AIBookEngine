Cambridge Collections includes short story and non-fiction collections as  well as themed, mixed-genre anthologies. Each title is designed to excite  students' interest and introduce them to a wide variety of genres, styles  and voices. A lively range of activities is integrated with the texts and  take an active approach to reading - including many ideas for speaking and  listening and drama work. Each anthology is supported by a Teachers'  Resource CD-ROM, providing a bank of differentiated, editable worksheets  plus stimulus and contextual material such as photographs and audio clips,  for use with an interactive whiteboard or individual computers.
