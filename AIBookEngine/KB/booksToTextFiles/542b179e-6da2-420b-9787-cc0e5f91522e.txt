ALICE'S PUZZLE ADVENTURES IN WONDERLANDInspired by Lewis Carroll's classic children's book, Aleksandra Artymowska's stunning scenes are packed with references to the original, from a mad hatter's tea party, to lines of marching playing cards. Readers must riddle their way from one page to another, cracking clues, finding hidden objects and meandering through mazes.
