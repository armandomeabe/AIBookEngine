A brand new workbook to accompany the coursebook and audio CD, in a  three-level lower secondary ESL programme designed to boost students'  abilities and confidence in English. Each unit is divided into sections  dealing with different aspects of language and skills development, including  grammar, language, vocabulary, study and research skills, etc. Attractively  illustrated and presented and thoroughly researched, this resource will give  a perfect grounding in ESL for those requiring either a stand-alone course  or preparation for the IGCSE or an equivalent qualification.
