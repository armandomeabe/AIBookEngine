Maribella is different from other children - she has a humpback. The people in her village say she brings bad luck, and they don't let her children near her. But Maribella's mother Luz tells them, 'My daughter has a big heart. One day she will do something special.' And one day she does, in a way that the villagers will never forget. Set in the mountains of Peru, this is a magical story of sadness and hope, kindness and courage
