Engaging content and a strong focus on grammar and vocabulary combine to  make this course a hit with both teachers and students. This Workbook  provides extra language and skills practice for use both in the classroom or  at home. It also includes extra vocabulary exercises corresponding to the  expanded lexical sets in the Student's Book 'Vocabulary bank'. 'Study Help'  and 'Skills Tips' sections give learners extra support and guidance. The  audio content to accompany the workbook is included on the Student's Book  DVD-ROM and on the Class Audio CDs.
