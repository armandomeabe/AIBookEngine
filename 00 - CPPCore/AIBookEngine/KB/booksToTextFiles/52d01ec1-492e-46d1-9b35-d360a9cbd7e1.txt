THE ONE WITH THE HOLIDAY DISASTER!*The twelfth laugh-out-loud, fully illustrated bestselling Wimpy Kid book!*Greg Heffley and his family are getting out of town.It's freezing cold at home and his parents are stressed about Christmas, so they decide the whole family are going to escape to a tropical island!A few days in paradise SHOULD do wonders for Greg and his frazzled family.But this version of paradise includes sun poisoning, stomach troubles and venomous creatures.Can their trip be saved, or will this island getaway end in disaster?
