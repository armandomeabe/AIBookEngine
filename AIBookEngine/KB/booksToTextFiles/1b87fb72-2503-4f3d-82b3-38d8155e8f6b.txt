Four complete past papers from University of Cambridge ESOL Examinations.  Cambridge BEC Preliminary 3 contains four complete practice tests for the  Cambridge BEC examination from University of Cambridge ESOL Examinations  (Cambridge ESOL).     These past examination papers provide the most authentic exam preparation  available. They allow candidates to familiarise themselves with the content  and format of the examination and to practise useful examination techniques.      The Student's Book also contains a comprehensive section of keys and  recording scripts, making it suitable for self-study. The Self-study Pack  contains the Student's Book with answers and Audio CD.
