ALI BABA AND THE FORTY THIEVES - EARLYREADS 5 (A1)Ali Baba's life changes on the day he sees forty thieves hiding their  treasure in a secret cave and hears the thieves' leader say the words 'Open  Sesame' to get into the cave. But before Ali and his family can enjoy the  riches, Ali finds there are many dangers they have to face.Adapted by Catherine E. WhiteLanguage ENGLISHGenre Fairy tale & FantasyLevel A1Examinations Movers
