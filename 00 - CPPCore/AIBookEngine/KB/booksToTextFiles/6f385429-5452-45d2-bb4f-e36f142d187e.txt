The Big Surprise! Teacher's Resource Pack provides a rich variety of engaging resources to captivate and inspire classes.The pack includes the Big Surprise! box which can be used at the start of the lesson to reveal the colourful flashcards that make learning vocabulary fun. The large, full-colour story cards with helpful comprehension questions on the back help tell the stories, and posters revise core vocabulary. The Big Surprise!
