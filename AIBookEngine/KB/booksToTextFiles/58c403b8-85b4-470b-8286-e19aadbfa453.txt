English Unlimited is a six-level goals-based course for adults. Centred on  purposeful, real-life objectives, it prepares learners to use English  independently for global communication. The Self-study Pack consists of a  Workbook with DVD-ROM. It enables learners to consolidate and build upon  work done in class.    The Workbook provides extra 'Explore Writing' tasks plus a complete 'Explore  Reading' syllabus, as well as 'Over to you' exercises allowing  personalisation of language practice.    In addition to over 300 interactive practice activities, the DVD-ROM  includes video for every unit featuring international speakers, documentary  clips, a self-recording and playback tool for pronunciation practice, and  customisable 'QuickCheck' tests to monitor progress.
