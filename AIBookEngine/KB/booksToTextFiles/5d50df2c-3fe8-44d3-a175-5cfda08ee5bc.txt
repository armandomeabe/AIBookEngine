Pandy the Panda is a three-level English language course consisting of class  books, activity books and teacher's books designed to feature topics used in  kindergarten in L1 as the axis around which the course rotates. Young  learners will be captivated by the adventures of Pandy, his friends and  their playhouse. It also motivates children through entertaining chants,  songs, pen-to-paper activities, games, puzzles, stickers, stories, etc.
