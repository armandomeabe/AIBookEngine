This new course provides students and teachers with current, meaningful, and  practical activities along a theme based approach to help students to  develop skills, gain confidence and enjoy the study of English. Each book  provides reading, writing, listening and speaking activities that support  the development of skills, knowledge, values and attitudes. Students will  find the content and activities motivating and relevant to many areas of  their study. Each of the four books includes a CD for listening skills and  activities and offers suggestions for how to integrate English with other  subjects. With internationally minded content and opportunities for enquiry  based learning, this series provides lively and colourful resources for a  wide range of students.
