MORE! is a bright new four-level lower-secondary course from a highly  respected author team. Each level of MORE! contains 80-90 hours of class  material. With dedicated reading, culture, grammar, vocabulary, skills and  cross-curricular learning sections, plus a wide range of flexible  components, you really do get more with MORE!     The Workbook with Audio CD contains exercises for extra practice in reading,  vocabulary, listening, grammar, and a 'Writing for your Portfolio' section.
