Terrible, unspeakable things happened to Sethe at sweet homa, the farm where  she lives as slave for so many years until she escaped to Ohio. Her new life  is full of hope but eighteen years later she is still not free. Sethe`s new  home is not only haunted by the memories of her past but also by the ghost  of her baby,who died nameless and whose tombstone is engraved with a single  word: Beloved
