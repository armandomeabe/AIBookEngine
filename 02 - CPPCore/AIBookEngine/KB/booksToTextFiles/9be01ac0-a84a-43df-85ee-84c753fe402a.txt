Comprehensive teacher resource packs from Cambridge ESOL to help teachers  who are preparing students to take a Cambridge English: Business  Certificates (BEC) Speaking test. The Speaking Test Preparation Pack for BEC  Vantage is a comprehensive teacher resource pack from Cambridge ESOL to help  teachers who are preparing students to take the Cambridge English: Business  Vantage (BEC Vantage) Speaking test. Written by experienced examiners, it  includes student worksheets with teacher's notes, two sets of candidate  visuals for Speaking test practice in class, and a DVD of students taking a  Business Vantage Speaking test so students can see what they will have to do  on the day of the test.
