JASON AND THE ARGONAUTS - GREEN APPLE 1 (A2)When he was very young, Jason's uncle took the kingdom of Iolkos from his  father, but now Jason wants it back. First he must find and bring back the  Golden Fleece. It is a dangerous mission but Jason and a crew of heroes set  sail on the Argo to the Black Sea. The Argonauts must battle against giants,  huge birds and sea monsters before their journey ends.Dossiers:Greek Pottery, Greek MythologyAdapted by Jennifer GascoigneLanguage ENGLISHGenre LegendLevel A2Examinations Key, Trinity
