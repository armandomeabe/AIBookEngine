Simple and ingenious, Pop-Out Stencil Art: Bugs shows you how to make a sparkly butterfly, amazing pictures, puppets and even a cute caterpillar clothes peg clip. Stencilling is a beguiling art, and is the perfect way to engage the imagination of budding artists. These stencil shapes will be sure to inspire young creatives to experiment. Pop-Out Stencil Art is the perfect way to show a young child how many ways there are to be creative. Featuring 15 easy to use pop-out stencils and 20 fun projects to make, these books will be sure to keep hands busy for hours. Each spread has three different sizes of pop-out stencils and creative ways to use them. The stencils can be reused time and again, and stored back in their nested shapes inside the book.
