Learn English with Dora the Explorer is a 3-level, pre-primary course that guides students as they take their first steps into the world of English, together with the adventurous Dora the Explorer.Take your students on a language-learning journey with activities, songs, and videos that will build confidence and develop the skills needed for primary school.Teaching with Learn English with Dora the Explorer is fun and effortless with a flexible package of resources to support your classes.The Phonics and Literacy strand offers extra support for vocabulary and pronunciation and material including Teacher's Guide, audio, and printable flashcards can be downloaded via the access code in the back of the Phonics and Literacy Book.Language Level: Pre-A1
