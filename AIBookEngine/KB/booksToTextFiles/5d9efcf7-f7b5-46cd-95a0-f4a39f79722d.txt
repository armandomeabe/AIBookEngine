*The seventh laugh-out-loud, fully illustrated bestselling Wimpy Kid book!*Greg Heffley's school is holding a Valentine's Day dance, and it's turned his world upside down.It's every man for himself as everyone scrambles to find a date for the big day, and Greg WON'T be left partner-less.His best friend, Rowley, doesn't have any prospects either but that's a small consolation...To make matters worse, his Uncle Gary has moved in and is taking over the whole house.
