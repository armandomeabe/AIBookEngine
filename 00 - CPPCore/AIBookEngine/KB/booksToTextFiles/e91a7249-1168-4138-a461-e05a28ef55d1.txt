Film, TV, and Music  Multi-level Photocopiable Activities for Teenagers    Containing over 40 activities specifically designed for teenagers, this is  an ideal photocopiable resource for busy teachers. The activities cover  three popular themes - Film, TV and Music - and they provide graded  supplementary material for three different levels, elementary, intermediate  and upper-intermediate, all in one book. Based on the theory of multiple  intelligences, there is a variety of tasks including role-plays, quizzes,  board games and debates which practise all four skills in a fun way. Each  activity is supported by step-by-step teacher's notes and extension ideas  ready to photocopy and go!
