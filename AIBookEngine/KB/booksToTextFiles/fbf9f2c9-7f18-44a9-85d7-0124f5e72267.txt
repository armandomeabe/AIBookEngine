I WANT TO BE YOU - GREEN APPLE 1 (A2)Holly and Rosie are cousins but they are very different. When the school  basketball team fails to win the championship because Holly misses a  penalty, Holly discovers just how cruel her cousin can be. Holly always  thought that Rosie had everything; lots of friends and lots of money to buy  lots of designer clothes. But then she meets a old lady at a fair and soon  finds out that things aren't always what they seem!Dossier:Travelling FunfairsAuthor Andrea M. HutchinsonLanguage ENGLISHGenre Human interestLevel A2Examinations Key, Trinity
