Great Minds is a six-level English course for the six primary school years. The syllabus covers the official curriculum and the basic competences developed in each unit have been highlighted. This is a syllabus that guarantees solid and progressive language acquisition, while maintaining skills balance.Stories are a highly valuable teaching tool and in addition to the rich linguistic input that they offer, Great Minds also uses stories as a vehicle for the illustration and discussion of values.Every unit offers extra activities for each lesson (for use either in class or at home) accessed by scanning the page including the Augmented Reality icon with a smart phone or tablet.
