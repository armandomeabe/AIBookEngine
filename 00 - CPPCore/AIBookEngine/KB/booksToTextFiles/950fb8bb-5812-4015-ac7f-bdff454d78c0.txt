Long ago, a powerful king named Midas lived in a place called Phyrgia. Midas  had a large kingdom, a beautiful palace, and a loving daughter. But the  thing Midas loved most of all was gold. One day, a god gave Midas the power  to turn everything he touches into gold. But was this power a blessing, or a  curse?     Our World is a six-level primary series in British English that uses fun and  fascinating National Geographic content, with stunning images and video, to  give young learners the essential English language, skills, and knowledge  they need to understand their world.
