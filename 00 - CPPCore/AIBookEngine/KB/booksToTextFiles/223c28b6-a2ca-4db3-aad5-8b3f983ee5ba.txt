Another adorable book from the wonderful Kate Toms, Lullabies will delight every child as they are lulled to sleep. Parents will love to read these books at bedtime to calm and relax their children and enjoy the beautiful hand-stitched illustrations that are Kate's signature style. Her cleverly adapted text is enjoyable to read, and features lullabies that are well loved across the world - perfect for children and parents alike. Including the well loved lullabies: Rock-a-bye Baby, Little Boy Blue, Sleep, Baby, Sleep, Wee Willie Winkie, Hey Diddle Diddle and All the Pretty Horses.
