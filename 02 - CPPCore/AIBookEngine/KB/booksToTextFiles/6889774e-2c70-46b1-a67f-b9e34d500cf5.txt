This newest edition of David Waugh and Tony Bushell's Key Geography provides the support you need to deliver the 2014 KS3 Programme of Study, with a focus on developing key geographical skills and techniques to prepare students for Key Stage 4. Foundations includes chapters on Weather and Climate and Kenya and Africa.FeaturesCase study topics support the requirements of the 2014 Programme of StudyKey skills chapters help embed key geographical techniques, preparing students for KS4Enquiry sections encourage students to apply their knowledgeClear progression through each topicAnswers to the Student Book activities can be found in the Foundations Teacher's Handbook
