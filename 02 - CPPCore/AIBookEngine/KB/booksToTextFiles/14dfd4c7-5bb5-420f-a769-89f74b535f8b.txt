Ja Klar!      German Course in 3 levels for the Primary School  Level A1/A2 of the Common European Framework of Reference  By G. Gerngross, W. Krenn, H. Puchta    The student's book consists of 10 separate themes that deal with themes in  the daily lives of the children, as well as three revision units and short  stories to dramatise. A comic strip story introduces the units and  furthermore, the rhymes, TPR activities, observation and logic games involve  the student, activating different skills. Completing the book are pages of  tests and an appendix of linguistic expressions.
