Cambridge English for Schools Practice Tests have been designed to  familiarise students with the level and format of the Cambridge English Key,  Preliminary and First (previously known as KET, PET and FCE) for Schools  examinations. Students can be assured that they will receive the relevant,  up-to-date, appropriate training to successfully undertake these tests. The  guidance and tips sections advise students on how to approach each part of  the examination and allow them to improve the skills required in the  examinations.     Key Features:  eight comlete practice tests; one with full guidance and two with  test-taking tips  a glossary of words that students may be nnfamiliar with  Cambridge English Key for Schools Teacher's book includes the overprinted  key and full recording scripts.
