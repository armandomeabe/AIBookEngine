Description: Ideal for the novice teacher, this book features sample lessons  with explanatory comments on the teaching techniques and methods used. Areas  covered include grammar, functions, vocabulary, pronunciation, and the four  skills, as well as planning, assessment and classroom management.    Key features  * Easy-to follow description of the language.  * Accessible lesson plans which provide a blueprint for teachers to use.  * A good way in to methodology that avoids a lot of technical jargon and  theory.
