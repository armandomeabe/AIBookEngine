After hearing about the "wild man of the forest," Felipe, Sandra, and Symon  spend their summer in search of the famous Bigfoot. As the reporters go deep  into the woods they wonder: Are the stories real? Does Bigfoot actually  exsist? Questions which have been asked before, except, this time, they may  actually find some answers.
